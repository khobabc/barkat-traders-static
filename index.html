About Us

Established in 2015, Barkat Traders is a general trading company based at Lahore, PAKISTAN . We are specialized in Importing, Processing/Sorting & Exporting used woolen clothes.

We are also exporting globally premium quality basmati rice from Pakistan.

Barkat Traders strives to become a significant player in used woolen clothes sorting, grading and sales business.

Our Services

We are general traders, importers and exporters specialized in sorting different type of wool sweaters like, Cashmere, Marino and Mixed Wool. Our operations cover following domains.

Import

Import used woolen sweaters from Europe & America. Sweaters are further processed to make them plausible in making of different wool products.

Processing

Assorted woolen sweaters are graded into diverse woolen content like Marino, Cashmere & mixed wool. Graded commodity is packed in bails for testing.

Export

After passing complex quality assurance tests, bails are packed and branded exported to local as well as multinational consumers across the globe.

Rice

Baasmati, a product of Pakistan

Pakistan is globally acclaimed for its aromatic and exquisite long grain baasmati rice production. We have a strong network of farmers and rice producers which process premium quality basmati rice ready for export. Basmati rice samples are available for testing and exported to different countries worldwide.
